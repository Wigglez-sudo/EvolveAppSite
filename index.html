Privacy-first by design

Your data never leaves your phone

Evolve runs entirely in your browser and installs to your home screen like a native app. There are no accounts and no servers logging your training. The only time anything leaves your device is when you choose to — saving an encrypted backup, or using the optional AI Coach with your own key.

Good to know

Questions, answered

Is it really free?+
Yes. Evolve is free, with no ads and no subscription. The only optional thing that could cost money is the AI Coach, and only if you choose a paid model with your own API key — the free options work too.
Do I need to create an account?+
No. There’s no sign-up and no login. You open the app and start using it — everything is stored on your device.
Where is my data stored?+
On your phone, only. Evolve has no server collecting your workouts or food. To move data between devices, you create an encrypted backup file and restore it with your password.
Does it work offline?+
Yes — once you’ve opened it once, Evolve works without a connection. You only need internet to get updates, download food packs, or use the AI Coach.
Will I lose my data if I close the app?+
No. Your data persists between sessions. It only clears if you reset the app, or delete the browser/app data — which is why the encrypted backup is worth setting up.
How do updates work?+
When a new version ships, Evolve shows an “Update available” banner. One tap updates it, and your data is preserved.
