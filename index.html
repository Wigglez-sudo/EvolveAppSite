Privacy-first by design

Your data never leaves your phone

Evolve runs entirely in your browser and installs to your home screen like a native app. No accounts, no servers logging your training. The only time anything leaves your device is when you choose to — an encrypted backup, or the optional AI Coach with your own key.

Good to know

Questions, answered

Is it really free?+
Yes. Evolve is free, with no ads and no subscription. The only optional cost is the AI Coach, and only if you pick a paid model with your own API key — the free options work too.
Do I need an account?+
No. There’s no sign-up and no login. You open the app and start — everything is stored on your device.
Where is my data stored?+
On your phone, only. To move data between devices, you create an encrypted backup file and restore it with your password.
Does it work offline?+
Yes — once opened, Evolve works without a connection. You only need internet for updates, food packs, or the AI Coach.
How do updates work?+
When a new version ships, Evolve shows an “Update available” banner. One tap updates it, and your data is preserved.
