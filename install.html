Get it on your phone

Install Evolve

No App Store needed. Open the app in your browser, then save it to your home screen — it runs full-screen and works offline.

1
Open the app in Safari Tap "⚡ Open the Evolve app" below. On iPhone this must be Safari — Chrome and other browsers don't support home screen install on iOS.
2
Tap the Share button The ⬆️ icon at the bottom of Safari (or top on iPad). If you don't see it, scroll up slightly.
3
Choose "Add to Home Screen" Scroll the share sheet down until you see Add to Home Screen, then tap it.
4
Tap "Add" to confirm Top-right of the prompt. The Evolve icon will appear on your home screen.
5
Open from the home screen icon Always launch from the icon (not Safari) so it runs full-screen and offline.
1
Open the app in Chrome Tap "⚡ Open the Evolve app" below in Chrome. Other browsers work too if Chrome isn't your default.
2
Look for the install banner Chrome may show an Install app banner at the bottom automatically — tap it and you're done.
3
Or tap the ⋮ menu Three dots, top-right of Chrome.
4
Choose "Install app" Tap Install app or Add to Home screen, then confirm in the dialog.
5
Open from your home screen Launch from the icon for the full-screen, offline experience.
